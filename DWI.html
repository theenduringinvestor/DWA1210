For Dawn,

A letter, our memories, and the songs that feel like us.

My love, another year has passed, and somehow my heart keeps finding new ways to fall for you. You are the quiet miracle woven into every part of my life — the softness in my days, the strength in my storms, and the peace I come home to. Loving you has never been just a moment; it’s been a lifetime unfolding, chapter by chapter, blessing by blessing.

Thank you for your patience, your laughter, your courage, and the light you carry so effortlessly. I’m grateful not just for the years we’ve shared, but for the way you’ve loved me through every season — with grace, with devotion, and with a heart that feels like home.

I promise to keep choosing you, to keep becoming the man you deserve, and to keep protecting the love we’ve built. Here’s to the life we’ve created, the memories we treasure, and the journey still ahead of us.

Forever yours, and always in awe of you.
