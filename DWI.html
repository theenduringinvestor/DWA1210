Letter

My love, another year has passed, and somehow my heart keeps finding new ways to fall for you. You are the quiet miracle woven into every part of my life — the softness in my days, the strength in my storms, and the peace I come home to.

Thank you for your patience, your laughter, your courage, and the light you carry so effortlessly. I’m grateful not just for the years we’ve shared, but for the way you’ve loved me through every season.

I promise to keep choosing you, to keep becoming the man you deserve, and to keep protecting the love we’ve built.

Forever yours, and always in awe of you.
